ESW 2017-2018

The ESW project aims to generate provincial and district level representative data on the economic and employment status of women in Punjab and establish a baseline for monitoring of Sustainable Development Goals 5 and 8 to support policy actions based on the evidence generated. The project aims to model evidence-based and targeted interventions to enhance women’s empowerment in Punjab and to reduce their vulnerability to various forms of discrimination and exclusion.

This website allows user to explore the collected data through interactive visualisations.
