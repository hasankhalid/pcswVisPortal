Guiding questions

How do indicators on socio-economic wellbeing for minority women compare to baseline indicators for all women in Punjab?

A separate survey, gathering data on socio economic indicators of minority women in Punjab was also conducted as part of the ESW survey 2018. The visualization in this section lets the readers compare selected indicators from the minority women survey with indicators from the baseline women’s survey. The goal is to inform policy design focused on empowering and safeguarding rights of minority women.

For a more detailed analysis, readers are encouraged to review the policy brief on minority women published by PCSW.
