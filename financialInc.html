Guiding questions

What are women’s attitudes towards VAW and how do they compare to men’s attitudes?

But while designing interventions that combat all forms of violence against women, discouraging attitudes that support or reinforce violence provides a major policy challenge. In ESW Survey 2018, data was collected on women’s attitudes towards physical and sexual violence by spouse.

Ever-married women were asked if their husband has a good reason to hit them in certain stated situations. Women’s responses at a district level can be investigated through heat map below. The grid has district on the vertical axis and different situations (coded by alphabets) on the horizontal axis. The percentage is encoded by color depth. The visualization also shows the actual incidence of physical violence in the districts through bubbles on the right so that women’s attitudes towards physical violence and incidence of physical spousal violence can be compared.
